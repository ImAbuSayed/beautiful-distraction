[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Your shadow dancing on the hardwood floor
I leave the keys inside the open door
The moonlight falling on your silhouette
This is a night I never will forget

[Verse 1]
We let the evening slowly fade away
With every quiet word you choose to say
The porch light flickers in the fading glow
We take it easy and we take it slow

[Pre-Chorus]
And time is standing still when you are near
My heavy breathing is the sound you hear
The shadows leaning on the painted wall
I step ahead and gladly take the fall

[Chorus]
You are a beautiful distraction in the dark
A sudden wildfire catching from a spark
A soft temptation I could never fight
You are my beautiful distraction in the night

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] (Keep pulling me near)
[Whispered] (There is nothing to fear)

[Verse 2]
I feel the steady pulse inside your chest
In this quiet corner where the weary rest
Your fingers running through my tangled hair
There is a heavy longing in the air

[Bridge]
The world outside is rushing in the cold
But you are everything I want to hold
